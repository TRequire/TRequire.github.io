The Royal Commission for Jubail and Yanbu (RC) seeks to unify the technical requirements of the Industrial Cities (ICs) within its jurisdiction within one year.

The RC directs the use of the “MasterSpec Program”.

When asked why MasterSpec?, the RC states that it is “easier”.
