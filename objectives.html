Objectives

    Create three sets of technical requirements that are:

  • Derived correctly from existing Saudi Arabian laws, regulations, and codes to create credibility
  • Consistent from project to project, regardless of who is on the design team, to create patterns for design reproduction
  • Easy to query for use in design activities
  • Easy to verify in a design
  • Easy to query for purchasing activities
  • Easy to verify in the field as a basis for progress reports and payment of invoices
  • Easy to modify for continuous improvement
  • Amenable to incorporation in Building Information Models (BIM) and in other databases such as those used for cost estimates and payment of invoices
